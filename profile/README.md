### 🌍 Our Mission
At **TechSoup**, we believe that technology is a powerful force for social good. We build the digital platforms and supply chains that connect non-profits, foundations, and libraries with the tech tools, resources, and knowledge they need to change the world.

### 🛠️ What We're Building
This GitHub organization serves as the hub for our open-source initiatives, community projects, and developer resources. 

- **Infrastructure:** Scalable solutions for global donation management.
- **Data for Good:** Tools to help civil society track impact and transparency.
- **Community Tools:** Resources to help NGOs modernize their digital workflow.
